Project Overview 
Build a complete Restaurant Table Booking, Ordering, Billing, and Management Web 
Application for Sri Vijaya Durga Restaurant. 
The system must not require Login or Registration for customers. 
The application should be fast, modern, responsive, mobile-friendly, and suitable for 
restaurants, cafes, food courts, and dining establishments.
